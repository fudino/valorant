About Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play multiplayer tactical first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games. Set in the near future, Valorant pits two teams of five players against each other in a strategic battle of attack and defense. Players choose from a diverse cast of agents, each with unique abilities that can turn the tide of battle.

Key Features

Unique Agents

Choose from a roster of diverse agents, each with their own set of abilities and playstyles.

Tactical Gameplay

Combine precise gunplay with strategic use of abilities for a deep, competitive experience.

Competitive Ranking

Climb the ranks and prove your skills in a robust competitive matchmaking system.

Regular Updates

Enjoy frequent updates with new agents, maps, and game modes to keep the experience fresh.

Gameplay Elements

Agents

Master a diverse cast of agents, each with unique abilities that can change the course of battle.

Weapons

Choose from a wide array of weapons, each with distinct characteristics and recoil patterns.

Maps

Compete across various maps, each offering unique layouts and strategic opportunities.

Economy

Manage your team's economy, deciding when to save and when to invest in powerful weapons and abilities.
